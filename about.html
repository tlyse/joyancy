关于弘盈

About Us

东莞市弘盈精密科技有限公司成立于2019年，为台湾上市集团公司所设立之子公司，专为服务国内各大、小企业所增设。本公司以制造模具为主轴，从设计、加工、开模、成型等一体化作业，全程配合客户生产与开发之所需，产品广泛应用于家电、手机、电脑、汽车、医疗器械、工具等行业。目前客户普及欧洲、美洲、日本及台湾地区，客户由苹果、惠而浦、奇异、TTI、Mabe、德国博世、VOLVO汽车、BMW汽车、日本牧田、台湾壹达、光宝等大型集团公司

本公司深耕本业，业务不断拓展，并沉淀了丰富的技术经验，长期以来重视人力资源的管理与合理利用，充分发挥人才技能的优势，激发员工的主动性与团队合作的意识。如今，公司拥有一批在精密零件制造方面具有丰富经验的资深技术人员。数年以来公司不断进行设备投资，增加引进高端先进设备，不断提高企业加工精度、品质与效率优势。

本公司秉承以客户为尊的宗旨，快速实业不断变化的需求，依据客户不同的需求，提供高品质、全制程精密模具及零件解决方案！

我们承诺：为客户提供最优质的产品和服务！

Dongguan Hongying Precision Technology Co., Ltd.,established in 2019,is a subsidiary of Taiwan listed group company, which is specially set up to serve domestic large and small enterprises. Our company takes mold manufacturing as the main axis, from the design, processing, mold opening, molding and other integrated operations, the whole process to meet the needs of customers' production and development, Products are widely used in home appliances, mobile phones, computers, automobiles, medical devices,tools and other industries. At present, customers are popular in Europe, America, Japan and Taiwan. Customers include Apple, Whirlpool, Singularity, TTI, Mabe, German Bosch, VOLVO automobile, BMW automobile, Japan Makita, Taiwan Delta, LITEON and other large group companies

The company is deeply rooted in the industry, business continues to expand, and has accumulated rich technical experience. For a long time, it attaches importance to the management and rational use of human resources, giving full play to the advantages of talents and skills, and stimulates the initiative of employees and awareness of team cooperation. Today, the company has a group of senior technicians with rich experience in precision parts manufacturing.

Over the years, the company has continuously invested in equipment, increased the introduction of high-end advanced equipment, and continuously improved the processing accuracy, quality and efficiency of the enterprise.

The company adheres to the customer-oriented purpose,fast response to changing needs, according to the different needs of customers,to provides high-quality,full process precision molds and parts solutions!

Our Commitment: To provide customers with the best quality products and services!
